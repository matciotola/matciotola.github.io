<h3>PhD Student @ University of Naples Federico II | Remote Sensing | Computer Vision | Pansharpening | Deep Learning</h3>

I am currently a PhD student at the <a href="https://itee.dieti.unina.it/index.php/it/dottorandi/xxxvi-ciclo-2020" target="_blank">University of Naples Federico II</a> and a member of the <a href="https://www.grip.unina.it/" target="_blank">GRIP Research Group</a>.
My research interests focus on techniques of Data Fusion for Remote Sensing imagery, in particular Super-Resolution and Pansharpening. I exploit the Python programming language, with libraries such as PyTorch, TensorFlow to achieve my goals. I'm trying to learn and understand much possible from the Machine Learning world, especially Supervised Deep Learning and Reinforcement Learning frameworks.

I received my MSc degree cum laude in Automation Engineering from University Federico II, with a thesis titled "Deep Learning methods for super-resolution and cloud removal for Sentinel-2 images".

I spend most of my free time cooking, shooting photos, travelling and playing with driving simulators. I like also listening to podcasts about actuality, gaming and vehicles. 

This blog has been open to interacting with other researchers and to better understanding how to improve my knowledge and sensibility in these themes.
